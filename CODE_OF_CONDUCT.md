# Code of Conduct

HyperionScan is dedicated to building a welcoming, harassment-free community for developers, researchers, and security professionals. This Code of Conduct sets expectations for interactions in GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and community channels.

## Our Standards

### Positive behaviors
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Respecting differing opinions, experiences, and backgrounds
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing appreciation for others' time and effort

### Unacceptable behaviors
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind
- Personal attacks or insulting/derogatory comments
- Trolling, deliberately disruptive behavior, or sustained flaming
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Sexualized language or imagery in any community space

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, or other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and may ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for behaviors that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers at **security@hyperionscan.dev**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

All community members are expected to comply with requests to stop unacceptable behavior. Failure to do so may result in immediate removal from community spaces.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and the public spaces where an individual is representing the project or its community. Examples include using an official project email address, posting via official social media accounts, or acting as a representative at an online or offline event.
